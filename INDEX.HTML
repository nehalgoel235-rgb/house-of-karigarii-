Our Story

House of Karigari was born from a deep desire to merge sustainability with social impact. We work with specially-abled children and young adults, training them in the art of jute craftsmanship — weaving, stitching, and designing premium eco-friendly products.

Every jute bag, storage basket, gift hamper, and home decor piece tells a story of dignity, skill, and hope. Our mission is to create livelihoods while protecting the planet.

Mission & Vision

🌱 Empower 500+ specially-abled artisans by 2027
🌍 Zero-waste, plastic-free, 100% biodegradable jute products
❤️ Craftsmanship meets compassion.
